Plant Care

Plant Care is a project that aims to revolutionize the way we monitor and maintain plant health. By leveraging advanced technologies, we provide real-time insights and solutions for optimal plant care. Our goal is to empower individuals and businesses to cultivate thriving plants with ease and efficiency.

About The Project


Plant disease detection using deep learing based on XAI

The project discusses the development of a deep learning and explainable AI. The proposed system solves some challenges that come with the traditional methods of detecting diseases, which were performed manually. This proposed paper discusses how CNNs can perform the image analysis automatically. The integration of LIME and SHAP into XAI techniques will be in the service of enhancing interpretability and, therefore, the transparency of model predictions for gaining user trust and adoption. Data collection, model development, mobile application development, and sensor system creation are all within the scope of this project for realtime plant health monitoring. Results are discussed for our first prototype, which includes a mobile app and a dashboard showing how this approach could improve yields, reduce losses, and contribute to food security in agriculture.
